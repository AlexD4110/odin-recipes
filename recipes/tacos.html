Tacos

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish made with a soft or hard tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients such as seasoned beef, chicken, or pork. The fillings are then topped with fresh ingredients like lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and salsa, offering a balance of flavors and textures. Tacos are quick to prepare, customizable, and perfect for any meal.

Whether you're making a classic beef taco or opting for a healthier alternative like grilled chicken or tofu, this recipe will guide you through creating the perfect taco. Enjoy your tacos with your favorite toppings and sides!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream (optional)
  • 1/4 cup salsa or taco sauce

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or chicken over medium heat until fully browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning to the meat, along with 1/4 cup of water. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Warm the taco shells or tortillas in the oven or microwave according to the package instructions.
  4. Assemble the tacos by filling each shell with the seasoned meat, shredded lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  5. Add optional toppings such as sour cream, salsa, or guacamole as desired.
  6. Serve the tacos immediately and enjoy!
